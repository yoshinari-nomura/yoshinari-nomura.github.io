## 来年の 5月6日はおやすみだよ :-)

ということは、schedule.smaple.jp に「5月3日、5月4日が日曜日の年
の5月6日」の日を加えれば良いので

(let ((date5-3 (mhc-date-new 2007 5 3))
      (date5-4 (mhc-date-new 2007 5 4))
      (year 2007))
  (while (< year 2032)
    (insert (format "%04d-05-03: %s\n%04d-05-04: %s\n"
		    year
		    (mhc-date-digit-to-ww-japanese-string
		     (mhc-date-ww date5-3) 'long)
		    year
		    (mhc-date-digit-to-ww-japanese-string
		     (mhc-date-ww date5-4) 'long)))
    (setq year (1+ year))
    (setq date5-3 (mhc-date-yy++ date5-3))
    (setq date5-4 (mhc-date-yy++ date5-4))))

とか

(let ((date5-3 (mhc-date-new 2007 5 3))
      (date5-4 (mhc-date-new 2007 5 4))
      (year 2007))
  (while (< year 2032)
    (when (or (= (mhc-date-ww date5-3) 0)
	      (= (mhc-date-ww date5-4) 0))
      (insert (format "%04d0506 " year)))
    (setq year (1+ year))
    (setq date5-3 (mhc-date-yy++ date5-3))
    (setq date5-4 (mhc-date-yy++ date5-4))))
