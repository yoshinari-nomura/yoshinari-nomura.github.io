CVSのcurrentを

(autoload 'mhc-mew-setup "mhc-mew")
(add-hook 'mew-init-hook 'mhc-mew-setup)

で使ってます。


|X-SC-Subject: =?iso-2022-jp?B?GyRCJUYlOSVIGyhC?=
|X-SC-Location: =?iso-2022-jp?B?GyRCPmw9ahsoQg==?=
|X-SC-Day: 20000817
|X-SC-Time:
|X-SC-Category: test
|X-SC-Priority:
|X-SC-Cond:
|X-SC-Duration:
|X-SC-Alarm:
|X-SC-Record-Id: <200008072249270042.kazu@test>
|Mime-Version: 1.0
|Content-Type: Text/Plain; charset=iso-2022-jp
|Content-Transfer-Encoding: 7bit
|
|本文

というようなデータで

|08/17 Thu             テスト [場所GyRCPmw9ahsoQg==?=]

のようにゴミが表示されます。
