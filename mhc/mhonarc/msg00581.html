もう一丁です。

patch 前
(ddate-mm-inc '(2000 1 31)) => (2000 2 31)
(ddate-mm-dec '(2000 5 31)) => (2000 4 31)

patch 後
(ddate-mm-inc '(2000 1 31)) => (2000 2 29)
(ddate-mm-dec '(2000 5 31)) => (2000 4 30)
